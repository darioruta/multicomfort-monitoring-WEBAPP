Our Mission

Nowadays, pollutants, personal bad habits and sedentary lifestyle, put the wellness on the edge and in most of the cases people are not aware about that. Several researchers spent an huge amount of time on understanding how building physical conditions could influence the behaviour and wellness of people who lives in, sometimes originating pathologies: the most common one is the so-called Sick-Building Syndrome.

We care about people's health and well-being, which is why we could not sit idly by.

Our Vision

We thought of engineering a mobile, portable, and easy-to-use device that makes the end user aware of a variety of comforts: light, acoustic, visual, and thermal comfort. This allows to have a facility always comfortable and, at the same time, reducing energy footprint emissions.

YES, ALL IN ONE!

Our Solution - McIo3 KIT

We designed our system to be totally modular by offering DATA PROCESSING and VISUALIZATION cloud services.
You can take advantage from the entire package of solutions (KIT, Algorithm and Visualization) to be aware of your own comfort. However, you can feel free to use your sensors and send us the data through API; We WILL COMPUTE and send back the comfort values for you. In addition, you can even choose to use another user interface (e.g GRAFANA).

COMPLETELY MODULAR, COMPLETELY COMFORTABLE!

Resilient, small and affordable KIT, equipped with several electronic sensors. It collects environemental data 24-h and send them via internet to a remote server. Here Comfort KPIs are computed and displayed in the dashboard. A Wi-Fi connection is required.

Our Solution - DATA PROCESSING & DASHBOARD

Comfort KPIs

Data retreived by our KITs are safely stored and processed aftewards, according to the latest comfort models present in scientific literature. Although Thermal Comfort is the most diffused comfort evaluator, it is not enough for us: we decided to show you even Visive Comfort and Acoustic comfort. You will be aware of your well beeing at glance since the cards change their color according to the KPIs computed.
